HOW IT WORKS


OUR MISSION:

We know you are busy— there are only so many hours in the day. You want to look fresh… You want to look clean… You want to look good.

Whether it's just a trim or a full haircut— Fresh Barber will work with your office to come to you and provide quick and easy no fuss haircuts on the go. We know your time is valuable. No more rushing to the barber down the street after work, only to find they close at 5pm. No more waiting 30 minutes at lunch for a simple haircut.


With Fresh Barber you can squeeze in a 20 minute cut in between meetings. Time's a wasting. Sign up with Fresh Barber today.

1. Login to see if we are currently serving your company.
